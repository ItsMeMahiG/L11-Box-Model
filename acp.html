Pizza, a globally beloved dish, originated in Naples, Italy, as a simple flatbread topped with various ingredients. The classic Neapolitan pizza, known as pizza Margherita, features tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and basil, representing the colors of the Italian flag.




Over time, pizza has evolved with countless regional variations and topping combinations, from the thin crusts of Rome to the deep-dish Chicago style. Its versatility, delicious taste, and ability to be customized make it a favorite food enjoyed worldwide, whether hot or cold, and in various settings.
